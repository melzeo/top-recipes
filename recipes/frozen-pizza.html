Frozen pizza

Description

How to make the best frozen pizza ever

Ingredients

  • Frozen pizza
  • Frozen cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Take the frozen pizza out of the packaging
  3. VERY IMPORTANT: Do NOT forget to remove any plastic wrapping
  4. Put more cheese on the pizza
  5. I said MORE!
  6. Ok, that's enough. Now put the pizza into the preheated oven and wait for ~15 minutes
  7. Take the pizza out of the oven and serve with some fresh basil leaves on top
  8. Enjoy!
